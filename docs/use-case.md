# AKC Use Case

## The Pain (35 words)

AI sessions are stateless amnesia. Lessons learned in one app launch—keyword strategies, screenshot layouts, timing—vanish into chat history. Junior specialists repeat senior mistakes. Team knowledge lives in individual heads, unstructured and lost.

## Demo Persona (50 words)

ASO Specialist at VNG Publishing launches games across JP, KR, VN, TH, PH. Each geo teaches lessons: hiragana long-tail keywords work in JP but fail in KR; ONE Store dual-submit is critical for KR's 28% install share; KakaoTalk CTAs lift D1 retention 8%.

Without AKC: trapped in one head. With AKC: `/recall` surfaces JP insight before KR launch. Pattern earns Gold tier (≥0.85) after 6+ successes. Team compounds knowledge visibly.

## Beyond ASO (60 words)

AKC's Pattern schema is domain-agnostic. **Engineering**: debug recipes, library gotchas. **Customer support**: resolution patterns, escalation rules. **Sales**: objection responses, deal patterns. Teams accumulate domain-specific patterns via `/remember` after each task.

## Why Compliance-Class Matters (40 words)

AKC runs on internal AgentBase—data never crosses to external Claude. Critical for DPO-mandated enterprises (VNG, banks, healthcare, GDPR). AKC is the **reference architecture**; VNG Publishing is the **reference deployment**.
